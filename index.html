Welcome to Glitzy Celebrations!

At Glitzy Celebrations, we believe that every event tells a story—one filled with dreams, passion, and the power of human connection. We don’t just manage events; we curate experiences that evoke joy, excitement, and lasting memories. From high-profile makeup seminars to corporate events, fashion shows, modeling showcases, and award ceremonies, our mission is to infuse every moment with genuine emotion, ensuring that your event leaves a lasting impact on every heart in the room.

Our team understands that behind every fashion show is the exhilaration of creativity, behind every corporate seminar is the pride of achievement, and behind every award ceremony is the celebration of hard work and dedication. We capture these emotions and translate them into moments that move, inspire, and resonate with your audience. Whether it’s the thrill of models gracing the runway or the powerful energy in a room filled with professionals eager to learn, we ensure your event embodies these emotions at every turn.

At Glitzy Celebrations, our attention to detail goes beyond logistics. We focus on creating an atmosphere where your guests feel the energy, excitement, and sincerity behind every element. From the careful selection of decor to the seamless flow of the event, we work tirelessly to ensure that the emotions you want to evoke are felt deeply by everyone in attendance.

We believe that emotions are at the heart of every successful event. It’s the laughter, the applause, the awe, and the joy that people remember long after the event is over. By connecting with these emotions, we turn ordinary gatherings into extraordinary memories. Let us partner with you to create an experience that transcends the ordinary—where every detail is thoughtfully designed, every moment is filled with emotion, and your vision is brought to life in ways that touch the hearts of your guests.

With Glitzy Celebrations, your event is more than just a production; it’s a heartfelt experience that will be remembered, cherished, and talked about for years to come. Let us make your next event not just a success, but a celebration of life’s most meaningful moments!

Vikas Gond

Founder, Glitzy Celebrations Varanasi

Vikas Gond is the visionary founder behind Glitzy Celebrations Varanasi, a premier event management company renowned for its elegant and grand-scale productions. A professional model since 2015, Vikas has built an impressive career, showcasing his talent and versatility on both national and regional stages.

His notable achievements in the fashion and event industry include:

  • Mr. Ranchi (Best Walk 2015) - Recognized for his exceptional runway presence.
  • Mr. Rohtas 2018 - Achieving prestigious titles in regional beauty pageants.
  • Director of Rohtas Got Talent 2023- Leading a successful talent showcase.
  • Director of National Beauty Seminar 2022 - Spearheading insightful discussions on beauty trends and industry standards.
  • Stage appearance with Mr. World Rohit Khandelwal (2017) - Sharing the spotlight with one of the industry’s most celebrated personalities.

With a passion for style, creativity, and leadership, Vikas continues to inspire through his modeling career and his ability to organize unforgettable events, making a mark in the fashion and event industries alike.

Anjani Kumar

Co-Founder & MFA, Banaras Hindu University

He has further honed his expertise through an extensive academic and artistic journey, highlighted by impressive accomplishments and contributions to the arts community in Banaras and beyond.

Academic and Professional Qualifications:

Artistic Contributions:
